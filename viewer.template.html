Why you can trust this. Everything happens in your browser, on your device — no upload, no server, no account, no internet needed. The page enforces a Content-Security-Policy that blocks all network connections, so it is technically incapable of transmitting your data anywhere. Verify it yourself: open your browser's developer tools, switch to the Network tab, then load your export — you'll see zero requests. The file is self-contained, so you can also disconnect from the internet first and it works exactly the same.
